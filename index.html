Roll up for the flavory tour and enjoy the sweetest experience in every bite!

The sweetest journey has half moon shape.

Nothing is more Argentinian than a cup of coffee accompanied by medialunas- a traditional Argentinian variety of medialunas. Perfect for breakfast or just a snack, our medialunas are made following a traditional recipe and using high-quality ingredients.Beware, they’re not your typical croissant: the soft dough holds the sweetness that is perfectly balanced by a hint of citrus. Eat them plain or add your favorite topping and enjoy the sweetest ride of your life!

Homemade with fresh ingredients and a sprinkle of love!

The secret behind the best medialunas lies in carefully selecting the ingredients. That is why we only use high-quality, fresh ingredients. Each bite brings up the buttery flavor, and the golden color comes from the fresh eggs and sugar glaze. We make our medialunas after a traditional recipe. That’s why our medialunas taste like the ones in Argentina, only they are made with the best ingredients in Copenhagen! Discover our products, and choose from different kinds of toppings: dulche-de-leche or custard cream. Or simply enjoy the authentic flavors of a traditional medialuna.
