Ordering process

1

Pre-order so nothing goes to waste

We know pre-ordering can sometimes be a nuisance. But to make sure we never waste any resources, we only work with pre-orders. That also helps us ensure our products are always fresh and full of flavor. All orders must be placed at least 24 hours before the pick-up date. When you place an order, you can also customize your box. Check out our offers or make your combo!

2

Shop our combos or make your own

Not all medialunas are the same. And not everybody likes the same things. That’s why we want to offer you the opportunity to choose whichever flavors you like. We also offer different toppings and vegan options. Our small boxes include six pieces, and the big ones twelve. You can mix and match your favorites. But if you feel uninspired, we have made some combos for you!

FIND YOUR COMBO CUSTOMISE YOUR ORDER
3

Choose your pick-up point

Our business is more than just about food. It is about connecting people and encouraging them to explore different cultures. That is why we have chosen pick-up as our primary delivery method. This way, we can meet our clients and help them see beyond the product, creating a meaningful experience for them. Because socializing is an important part of discovering and understanding a new culture, we want to always keep in touch with our customers. Choose a pick-up point that’s convenient for you and let’s meet!

LEARN MORE
4

Our partners can get your order home for you

Because we want to bring our medialunas closer to you, we have partnered with food delivery companies. You can now have our products delivered right to your house, thanks to our partners, Bolt and Just eat. All you have to do is choose the preferred delivery method when you place your order. After that, you just have to wait and make sure you’re home when they’ll ring your bell.
