Our story

A piece of Argentina in the heart of Copenhagen.


Hi! I am Lucas, the man behind Entremasas. I am a professional pastry chef from Argentina, and I moved to Copenhagen in 2020. That is when I started making traditional Argentinian sweets because I missed home. I shared them with my friends, and they fell in love with their taste and fluffiness. They always encouraged me to follow my passion and give more people the chance to try them. So I started using Whatsapp to sell medialunas to Argentinian people who missed their home.


But Entremasas is more than just nostalgic memories about home. Entremasas is about connecting people through food. Medialunas are perfect for a breakfast shared with family or over coffee with friends or colleagues. Medialunas bring people together because they are made to be shared.


When I prepare the sweets, I follow a traditional Argentinian recipe, because I believe you can experience a culture through food. Medialunas are an Argentinian variety of croissants. What makes them different from typical croissants is their crescent shape. Their name comes from their shape, and medialuna literally translates to "half-moon".


Another thing that makes our medialunas stand out is the citrus flavor. The dough is made with zest from both lemons and oranges. That balances the sweetness and offers a unique flavor.
Discover their unique taste and let yourself be carried away!

Our business grew, and more and more people became curious about medialunas. That's how I started selling medialunas to people of different nationalities living in Copenhagen. My dream is for all people in Copenhagen to be able to taste a tiny piece of Argentina.

Now I am looking for ways how I can bring our medialunas even closer to you. Imagine going out for a coffee and ordering medialunas on the side. Now that would be a dream! So, if you are a cafe owner and would like to partner up, don't hesitate to contact me. I know that once you get a taste of our croissants, you'll be up for the journey!
